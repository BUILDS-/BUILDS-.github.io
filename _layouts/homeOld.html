What is BUILDS?


BUILDS is a student-run hackerspace that provides students with tools and resources for working on collaborative projects.


If you're looking for people enthusiastic about tech, security, electronics, and art, swing by MCS B26 at Boston University to check out our hackerspace! Access is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
